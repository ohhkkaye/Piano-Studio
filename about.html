About

Kathleen has been teaching for nearly a decade and has been playing both the violin and piano since she was 9 years old. She began her love for music at a young age of 2 where she used to watch her favorite TV shows and sing along to all the disney movies she could get her hands on. As soon as she hit 7 years old, she received a small keyboard and she would play any melodies that were on the radio. It was then that her mother realized she needed piano lessons.

Kathleen graduated from George Mason University's School of Music with a Bachelor of Arts in Music

In college she picked up the viola and the cello as additional instruments. As she continues to teach, she has found love for teaching the arts to young musicians.
